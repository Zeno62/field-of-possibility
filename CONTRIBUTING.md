## Core Principles

Please preserve the following principles when suggesting changes.

### 1. The Chinese Edition Is the Source Edition

The Chinese edition remains the source edition of the project.

English and other language versions should be reviewed against the Chinese source edition when accuracy is in question.

---

### 2. Preserve the Two-Layer Model

The framework uses a strict two-layer model:

**Layer 1: Pure Possibility / Awareness / Luminosity / All-Knowing**

**Layer 2: Field of Possibility / Total Manifestation / Totality**

Please avoid suggestions that collapse these two layers into one.

---

### 3. Awareness Is Not Consciousness

One of the most important distinctions in the project is:

> Awareness is not consciousness.

Consciousness belongs to manifestation.  
Awareness points to the source layer.

Please avoid language that treats Awareness as attention, thought, mind, inner observer, self-awareness, or psychological consciousness.

---

### 4. Pure Possibility Is Not Ordinary Possibility

Pure Possibility does not mean ordinary possibility, probability, future options, potential events, or a beginning in time.

It points to the source layer.

Please avoid translations or explanations that reduce Pure Possibility to ordinary potentiality.

---

### 5. Do Not Turn the Framework Into Religion or Self-Help

This project is not:

- a religion;
- a spiritual doctrine;
- a self-help system;
- a motivational philosophy;
- a mystical proof system;
- a closed belief structure.

Suggestions should help preserve the framework as an open philosophical tool for observation.
