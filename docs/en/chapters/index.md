# English Chapters  
## Field of Possibility

This section will contain translated and adapted English chapters of **Field of Possibility / 可能性网络**.

The Chinese edition remains the source edition of the project.  
All English chapters are translated from, or based on, the Chinese source version.

Current source version:

**v4.0.0 Official Chinese Edition**

---

## Translation Status

The English chapter translation is currently in progress.

Status:

**Draft preparation**

Each translated chapter may have one of the following statuses:

- **draft** — initial translation, not yet reviewed;
- **reviewed** — reviewed by contributors;
- **official** — accepted as an official English version;
- **outdated** — source version has changed and translation needs updating.
