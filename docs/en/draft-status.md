## What “Draft Preparation” Means

Draft preparation means:

- the English chapters are readable;
- the core structure has been translated and adapted;
- key terms follow the current translation map;
- the chapters may still need language review;
- terminology may still be refined;
- contributors may still suggest improvements;
- future revisions may be made before an official English release.

The current English draft should be treated as a working version.

---

## What This English Draft Is For

The English draft is intended to:

- introduce the framework to international readers;
- provide a readable English entry point;
- support discussion and feedback;
- help translators understand the core structure;
- make key concepts accessible before a formal English release;
- invite careful review from readers and contributors.

It is not intended to replace the Chinese source edition.

## Review Priorities

Future review may focus on:

- conceptual accuracy;
- terminology consistency;
- English readability;
- avoiding religious or self-help framing;
- preserving the two-layer model;
- preserving the distinction between Awareness and consciousness;
- preserving the distinction between Pure Possibility and ordinary possibility;
- preserving the central mechanism of **Mistaking the Apparent for the Real**.
