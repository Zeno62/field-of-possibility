## Translation Principles

The English edition should preserve:

- the distinction between Awareness and Consciousness;
- the distinction between Pure Possibility and Field of Possibility;
- the meaning of **认假为真**;
- the meaning of Single-Frame Interruption;
- the non-religious and non-self-help boundary of the project.

The English edition should not turn this framework into:

- a religion;
- a mystical system;
- a self-help doctrine;
- a success philosophy;
- a quantum mysticism framework.
