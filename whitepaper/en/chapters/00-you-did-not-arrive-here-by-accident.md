# 0｜You Did Not Arrive Here by Accident

Perhaps you simply opened a document.

But if you are willing to look back honestly at your own life experience, you may have already sensed something faint yet recurring: you are not living in complete freedom.

Certain fears, relationships, desires, frustrations, expectations, judgments, and senses of identity seem to repeat like scripts already written.

You may have changed cities, changed jobs, changed relationships, and changed environments, yet again and again you find yourself falling back into similar emotional structures.

This whitepaper does not rush to deny that feeling.

It treats that feeling as an entrance.

The Field of Possibility attempts to answer a series of questions:

How does reality appear?  
How is the self generated?  
Why does suffering repeat?  
What is consciousness?  
What is life?  
Where does freedom occur?  
Is lostness the final destination?  
Is Return truly possible?

It is not a religious doctrine.  
It is not a conclusion of physics.  
It is not psychotherapy.  
It is not a doctrine of success.  
It is not a conspiracy theory.

It is closer to a tool for debugging thought.

What it truly seeks to point out is this:

> How we mistake manifested images for the real self; how we mistake thoughts for reality; how we mistake feelings for fate; and how we may withdraw from this mechanism.

More deeply:

> **Lostness is not the final destination. Return is not walking back from one place to another. Return is recognizing Total Knowing.**
