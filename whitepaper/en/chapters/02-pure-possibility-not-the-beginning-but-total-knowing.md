# 2｜Pure Possibility: Not the Beginning, but Total Knowing

Pure Possibility may also be called Awareness or Luminosity.

It is not matter.

It is not consciousness.

It is not psychological activity.

It is not a cognitive function.

It is not a subject’s awareness of an object.

It is not a simple collection of all possible paths in the universe.

Nor is it an “initial state” in the sense of time.

If we say that Pure Possibility is the source before all manifestation, the word “before” does not mean temporal sequence.

It refers to an ontological relationship.

More accurately:

> **Pure Possibility is the source-level Luminosity of Total Knowing.**

It is not “many possibilities.”

It is that by which all possibilities are possible.

It is not one part of the network.

It is the fundamental condition through which the network can exist at all.
