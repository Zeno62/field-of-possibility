# 4｜Time and Space: The Dimensionally Reduced Felt Experience of Local Life

The Mother Field of Possibility is not an object that exists within time and space.

On the contrary:

> **Time and space are the dimensionally reduced felt experience through which life, on local subnets, experiences the Totality of the Mother Field.**

Time may be understood as the serialized felt experience of a local life-node toward the relational structure of the Mother Field.

Space may be understood as the distanced felt experience of a local life-node toward the relational structure of the Mother Field.

The past is the node’s retrospective felt experience of certain sedimented Weight.

The present is the node’s focused felt experience of the current local state.

The future is the node’s predictive felt experience of structures not yet directly experienced.

But none of these are absolute divisions within the Mother Field as a whole.

Much suffering comes from a basic misrecognition:

The individual mistakes thoughts of the past for a truly existing past.

The individual mistakes predictions of the future for a future that is truly pressing in.

And the individual forgets that, apart from the present, nothing on the timeline can actually be grasped.

Whether the present is good or bad is the fruit of past causes.

But the past has already passed.

Whether the future is good or bad is caused by the present.

Therefore, in the present, one only needs to choose for the goal and work for the goal.
