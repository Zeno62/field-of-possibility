# 5｜Consciousness: Logical Inertia Formed at Low Probability

Consciousness is not Awareness.

Awareness is source-level Luminosity.

Consciousness is a manifested structure within the Mother Field.

More accurately:

> **Consciousness is a logical inertia that forms at low probability and continuously reinforces itself after combinations of possibility become complex to a certain degree.**

Consciousness continuously receives input, trains itself, forms models, gives feedback, corrects itself, reinforces paths, solidifies preferences, forms judgments, and maintains its own continuity.

Human consciousness is like this.

Social ideology is like this.

AI, at the functional level, has also already reproduced this mechanism quite well.

The chain of consciousness usually looks like this:

```text
Collective Weight and Individual Weight affect the current node
↓
consciousness arises
↓
consciousness begins to judge, interpret, associate, and predict
↓
feeling appears
↓
feeling pushes further narration
↓
narration pushes automatic action
↓
the old path is reinforced again
```

But the chain of consciousness is not impossible to interrupt.

When consciousness arises, see consciousness.

When feeling appears, see feeling.

This is the basis of Single-Frame Interruption.
