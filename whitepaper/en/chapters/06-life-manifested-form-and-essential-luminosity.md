# 6｜Life: Manifested Form and Essential Luminosity

Previously, life could be defined in this way:

> **Life is the form that appears when Luminosity is manifested within a subnet of consciousness.**

This definition is valid at the level of manifestation.

But at a deeper level, another layer must be added:

> **The essence of life is not this form, but Luminosity itself.**

At the level of manifestation, life is the form in which Luminosity appears within a certain subnet of consciousness.

Human beings, animals, bodies, perception, emotion, desire, suffering, memory, death, relationship, and growth all belong to the manifested forms of life within the Mother Field.

But at the essential level, life is not the body.

It is not consciousness.

It is not the continuity of experience.

It is not the biological brain.

It is not a carbon-based structure.

It is not a complex system.

The essence of life is Luminosity.

The externally manifested Mother Field is only an image in the mirror.

The body is an image.

Consciousness is an image.

Time is an image.

Space is an image.

Relationship is an image.

Birth and death are images.

Even the Totality of the Mother Field is a mirror-image manifested by Luminosity.

So a more accurate expression is:

```text
Essence of life = Luminosity
Ordinary life = the reflected form of Luminosity within a subnet of consciousness
Manifested Mother Field = mirror-image
```

When a person mistakes the body, identity, experience, success and failure, relationship, emotions, or activities of consciousness for life itself, that person has already entered Mistaking the Apparent for the Real.

Life is not the image.

Life is the Luminosity that illuminates the image.
